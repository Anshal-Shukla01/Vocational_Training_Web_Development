#COMBINATOR


In style 


1.Descendent Combinator - Reflects to all the tags inside the class.
          |
          v
.classname tag{

     --Styling Property--
}


2.Child Combinator - Observed : It doesnt reflected the color to the child div of it.Applied to direct Child only.

          |
          V
.classname>tag{

     --Styling Property--
}

3.Sibling Combinator - INstead of that class it adds to its sibling but not after it.
          |
          V
.classname+tag{
     --Styling Property--
}

4.Subsequent Sibling Combinator - It adds the styling to all the siblings i.e. all below that class.
           |
           V
 .classname~tag{

     --Styling Property--
}
