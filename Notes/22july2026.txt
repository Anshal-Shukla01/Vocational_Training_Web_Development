#PRIORITY
As we know the class styling is prioritised over global styling so to get global styling to be implemented !important is used after a space in the end of styling.


#GRADIENT

1.Linear Gradient:

background-image: linear-gradient(--ALIGNMENT--,COLOR1,COLOR2,...);           //ALIGNMENT - TO TOP/TO BOTTOM/TO RIGHT/TO LEFT/TO BOTTOM RIGHT/45DEG 

2.Repeating Linear Gradient:

repeating-linear-gradient(color1,color2 %, color3 %,...)

3.Radial Gradient:

background-image: radial-gradient(color1,color2,... );

4.Repeating Radial Gradient:

repeating-linear-gradient(color1,color2 %, color3 %,...)
