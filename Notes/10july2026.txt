#BACKGROUND IMAGE

Inside Style Tag or Style file
background-image:url(" ---- ");

#BACKGROUND REPEAT

-Remove the background repetition
        background-repeat: no-repeat;
-Repeat Vertically
        background-repeat: repeat-y;
-Repeat Horizontally
        background-repeat: repeat-x; 


#BACKGROUND SIZE
       1. background-size:cover;  - fills in the overall container .
       2. background-size:contain;  - fits in the container. 


#BACKGROUND POSITION
    
    background-position : center;
    - To Not Align in Center but to position it 
    background-position : --alignment-- --size--px;
