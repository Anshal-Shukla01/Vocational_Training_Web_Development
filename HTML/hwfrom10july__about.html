The Ananda Imperial Hotel

The Aananda Imperial Bilaspur is a 4 star luxury hotel in the heart of the city and has defined hospitality in the truest sense of the Indian tradition of “Atithi Devo Bhaba”. It is Eco friendly and is a full service hotel that has heralded a new standard of deliverance with responsibility in the hospitality scenario of Chattisgarh. It is an extremely business friendly hotel situated in a lively area and within short walking distance from the main business hub of Bilaspur. Also close by is a shopping mall and Movie Multiplex.The property is the preferred choice of accommodation for all travellers with guest satisfaction being achieved over and over again. Repeat footfall is the yardstick to the level of satisfaction at the hotel. It has successfully built an undisputed reputation of comfortable stays as well as outstanding cuisines. The hotel rose to fame over the last 8 years and successfully leads F&B offerings and its unique promotion throughout the city…it remains the choice numero uno over higher star ventures.The Aananda Imperial is the leading venue for all major corporates. It ensures guest hassle free stays and comfort in all aspects from checking in/out, transfers to and from airport and railway terminus. Maximum guest satisfaction has been the objective. The service at the hotel is discerning to the minutest of details. The hotel has cool, open spaces that are perfectly complemented by its warm and intimate generosity. It comprises of 70 tastefully decorated rooms, banquet halls and a lawn, which can accommodate anywhere from 25 to 2000 people along with a round the clock dining facility at The Cafe. The elegantly done contemporary Multicuisine Restaurant Gourmet – Global Cuisines, offers exotic cuisines from across the continents. The hotel also houses a Gymnasium and a Business Centre located on the ground and level one respectively.
